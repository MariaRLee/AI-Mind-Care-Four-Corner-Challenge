# 四角號碼檢字法的歷史  
# History of the Four-Corner Method

## 中文

四角號碼檢字法由王雲五先生創制，目的是讓使用者即使不知道漢字讀音，也不確定部首，仍能根據字形快速查字。王雲五於1925年發表「號碼檢字法」，1926年出版《四角號碼檢字法》。

取碼順序為：

1. 左上角
2. 右上角
3. 左下角
4. 右下角

四個角的筆形分成0–9十類，再組成四位號碼。傳統口訣是：

> 一橫二垂三點捺，  
> 點下帶橫變零頭；  
> 叉四插五方塊六，  
> 七角八八小是九。

例子：

- 端＝0212
- 教＝4844
- 十＝4000
- 大＝4003
- 國＝6015

同一筆通常不重複取碼；外框字的下角可能取框內筆形。同一四碼可能對應多個漢字，因此部分字典加入第五位「附角」。

四角號碼不只是查字方法，也結合視覺辨識、空間觀察、記憶與數字編碼，適合轉化為漢字學習與腦力挑戰。

## English

The Four-Corner Method was developed by Wang Yun-wu to help users find a Chinese character from its visual form, even when they did not know its pronunciation or radical. He introduced the numerical indexing method in 1925 and published *The Four-Corner Method* in 1926.

The coding order is:

1. upper-left
2. upper-right
3. lower-left
4. lower-right

The corner shapes are classified into digits 0–9 and combined into a four-digit code. Examples include:

- 端 = 0212
- 教 = 4844
- 十 = 4000
- 大 = 4003
- 國 = 6015

A stroke is generally not coded twice. For enclosure characters, lower corners may be taken from inside the frame. Because several characters may share the same four-digit code, some dictionaries use an optional fifth supplementary-corner digit.

The method combines visual recognition, spatial observation, memory, and numerical coding, making it valuable for character learning and cognitive challenges today.
