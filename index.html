1 先認識四角號碼 ｜ Learn the Four-Corner Method

四角號碼是依漢字外形查字的方法：看左上、右上、左下、右下四個角，再把筆形轉成0–9。 The Four-Corner Method reads a character by looking at the upper-left, upper-right, lower-left, and lower-right corners, then converting those shapes into digits 0–9.

範例：大 ｜ Example: 大 (dà, big)

左上是交叉形「4」，右上與左下沒有另取筆形為「0」，右下是點捺形「3」。 Upper-left is a crossing shape “4”; upper-right and lower-left are “0”; lower-right is a dot/downstroke shape “3”.
💡 初學提醒：Beginner note: 同一筆通常不重複取碼，外框字與複合字另有細則。本遊戲先使用常見、適合入門的漢字，並以四位基本碼練習。 A stroke is generally not coded twice, and enclosure characters have extra rules. This beginner game uses curated common characters and the basic four-digit code.
