四角號碼檢字法的歷史 History of the Four-Corner Method

四角號碼檢字法由王雲五先生創制，讓使用者即使不知道漢字讀音，也不確定部首，仍能根據字形快速查字。 The Four-Corner Method was developed by Wang Yun-wu so that a character could be located from its visual form—even when its pronunciation and radical were unknown.

1925 王雲五發表「號碼檢字法」。 Wang Yun-wu introduced his numerical character-indexing method.
1926 《四角號碼檢字法》出版，方法逐漸應用於字典與資料編排。 The Four-Corner Method was published and became a practical tool for dictionaries and information organization.

核心方法 Core Method

①左上Upper-left → ②右上Upper-right → ③左下Lower-left → ④右下Lower-right

把四個角的筆形分成0–9十類，再組成四位號碼；同一組號碼可能對應多個漢字，部分字典會加入第五位「附角」協助區分。 The shapes at the four corners are classified into digits 0–9 and combined into a four-digit code. Because several characters may share a code, some dictionaries add an optional fifth “supplementary corner” digit.

展開傳統口訣、例子與重要規則 Show the traditional mnemonic, examples, and key rules

傳統口訣 Traditional Mnemonic

一橫二垂三點捺，
點下帶橫變零頭；
叉四插五方塊六，
七角八八小是九。

The digits represent ten broad corner shapes: head, horizontal, vertical, dot, crossing, piercing, square, corner, split-eight, and small-shaped strokes.

今日的學習價值 Learning Value Today

四角號碼不只是查字方法，也結合視覺辨識、空間觀察、記憶與數字編碼，因此很適合轉化為漢字學習與腦力挑戰。 The method is more than a lookup system: it combines visual recognition, spatial observation, memory, and numerical coding, making it well suited to character learning and cognitive challenges.

先認識四角號碼 ｜ Learn the Four-Corner Method

四角號碼是依漢字外形查字的方法：看左上、右上、左下、右下四個角，再把筆形轉成0–9。 The Four-Corner Method reads a character by looking at the upper-left, upper-right, lower-left, and lower-right corners, then converting those shapes into digits 0–9.
